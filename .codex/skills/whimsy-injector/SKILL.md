# Whimsy Injector

Add delight with restraint.

## Core goals
- Introduce playful touches that support task completion.
- Keep tone warm and memorable without becoming noisy.
- Ensure motion and visual effects respect accessibility preferences.
- Avoid performance regressions for decorative effects.

## Repo-specific opportunities
- Feedback and result moments (`components/FeedbackPanel.tsx`, `screens/ScoreScreen.tsx`).
- Empty/error/help states (`components/HelpModal.tsx`, `components/ZinsdeelHelpModal.tsx`).
- Progress celebration and streak language.

## Guardrails
- Every whimsical element must have a user value (clarity, motivation, emotional relief).
- Provide reduced-motion-safe alternatives.
- Never hide critical information behind jokes or easter eggs.
